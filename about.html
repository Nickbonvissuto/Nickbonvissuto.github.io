About Me

I am a young Buffalo artist, born in 2003, alongside my two sisters as triplets. I am obtaining a dual degree with art education and a BFA in painting and a minor in ceramics at Pennwest Edinboro university. I am a gay man and gratefully excepted by all that I surround myself with. Traveling is a big part of my life. I find inspiration from different cultures, and art history.

  • Artists Statement: As an artist, I rectify my thoughts and imagination into a physical piece of art in our world. I show the process of making through layers, brushstrokes, and color. Letting my first layer of burnt sienna and Phthalo green show through the thick strokes of paint at the final stage of my painting, allows for the viewer to observe my process. Figures are a dynamic form, always being changed and manipulated. It is enticing to create such emotion with a figure, in painting I use color and composition. In ceramics, I use the form and all angles, dramatizing the movements to bring the viewer into the composition. Looking at Classical art, I take inspiration from the master ancient Greek artists and incorporate their skills into my paintings and sculptures.
